ISRO Scientists Salaries: A Look at Earnings for Freshers and Top Experts

ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) is one of the premier space agencies in the world. Working here as a scientist is a prestigious job, but it also comes with financial rewards. Below is a detailed look at the salaries for freshers and top experts at ISRO.

Freshers Salaries

Freshers at ISRO typically earn within the range of INR 56,100 to INR 1,77,500 per month, depending on their grade and experience. This includes various allowances and benefits that come with the job.

Top Experts Salaries

Top experts and experienced scientists can earn up to INR 2.5 lakh per month or more. These figures reflect years of dedicated service and significant contributions to Indian space missions.

ISRO scientists enjoy several perks such as healthcare benefits, housing facilities, and educational allowances for children, making it a highly desirable career for many aspiring scientists.
