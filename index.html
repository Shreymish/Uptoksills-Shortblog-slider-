Axolotl
Native to Mexico, the Axolotl is known for its ability to regenerate limbs. It lives in freshwater lakes and is critically endangered.
Pangolin
Pangolins are nocturnal mammals covered in protective keratin scales and are found in Asia and Africa.
Saiga Antelope
Found in the steppes of Central Asia, the Saiga Antelope is recognized for its unique, bulbous nose.
