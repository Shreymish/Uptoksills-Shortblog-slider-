Axolotl

The axolotl is a neotenic salamander native to Mexico. It is known for its ability to regenerate limbs and other body parts.

Aye-Aye

The aye-aye is a nocturnal lemur found in Madagascar, recognizable by its unique middle finger used to tap on wood and locate insects.

Glass Frog

Known for its translucent skin, the glass frog is native to Central and South America, allowing a view of its internal organs.

Tardigrade

The tardigrade, or water bear, is a microscopic creature known for its resilience, surviving extreme conditions like space.

Pygmy Seahorse

These tiny seahorses blend seamlessly with coral reefs and are found in the Pacific Ocean, especially near Indonesia and the Philippines.

Narwhal

The narwhal, known as the unicorn of the sea, is a whale with a long tusk and is found in Arctic waters.

Yeti Crab

This deep-sea creature has hairy claws and is found near hydrothermal vents in the Southern Ocean.

Pink Fairy Armadillo

The smallest species of armadillo, the pink fairy armadillo is found in the sandy plains of Argentina.

Kakapo

The kakapo is a nocturnal, flightless parrot native to New Zealand, known for its strong, musky scent.

Saiga Antelope

The saiga antelope is characterized by its distinctive bulbous nose and is found in the steppes of Central Asia.
